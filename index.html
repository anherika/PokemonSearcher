Pokémon Searcher

What is the initiative?

PokéFind is made by fans, for fans!


It’s a Pokémon search tool to help you build the best team for competitive tournaments in Scarlet & Violet and Sword & Shield. Soon, we’ll cover all official formats!


Our goal is simple: make team building easier, more fun, and bring trainers together. Let’s play, share, and enjoy the game!
